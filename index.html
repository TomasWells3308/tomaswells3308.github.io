Hello and welcome to my portfolio site!

I am currently a junior gameplay programmer at Freejam working on Robocraft 2. Before that I graduated a BA(Hons) Gameplay Programming course at Falmouth Univerity with First-Class Honours. I have worked on multiple games, either alone or as part of a group of up to 12 people from other disciplines. I am open for any work!
